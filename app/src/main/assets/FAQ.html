REGISTRATION:

What is MyBat11?

MyBat11 is an Indian Cricket Fantasy Platform to play online game in which a virtual team of real cricket players is created and points are scored depending on how those players perform in real life matches. To win a contest, players must work towards attaining the maximum points and the highest rank on the leaderboard.

How do I register?

It’s very simple. Just enter your mobile/email Id & create a password or register instantly with your Facebook or Google+ account.

How many accounts can I create with the same Mobile no/email id on MyBat11?

You can create only one account with one Mobile no/email id on MyBat11. Creation of multiple accounts by a single user is strictly prohibited and violates our Fair Play policy.

GAME CENTER:

How do I play on MyBat11?

Easy! Just follow the following steps.

1 - Register/Log into MyBat11.com/app

2 – Select an upcoming match & choose the contest which is most suitable for you.

3 - Click on Join and/or Create your team of 11 players (including a Captain & Vice-captain) within an allocated virtual point of 100 credits from all the players in the match

4 – You can join any number of cash/free contests of your choice. Cash contests let you win cash and require an entry fee, for which we provide multiple payment methods such as Paytm, Credit Cards, Debit Cards & Net banking.

5 - Once the live match starts, your team starts earning points based on actual performances of the players selected by you. Final points, ranks and winners are declared after the end of the match

Can I select players from one side only?

A maximum of 7 players can be selected from each side, the remaining need to be from the other side.

How can I change my team name?

Sorry, you can’t change your team name once registered. Choose your team name wisely, it’s permanent.

How do I create multiple teams for the same match?

Multiple team option is enabled after creating your first team. You can create multiple teams by clicking 'Create Team 2’ above your team on the my contests page.

How many teams can I create for a single match?

You can create up to 10 separate teams for a single match.

Can I join the same contest using multiple teams?

You can join only one contest with one team. However, contests which display ‘Multi Entry’ on them allow you to join the same with multiple teams.

What are public & private contests?

Public contests are open for all to join; whereas private contests let you invite & challenge your friends in the same contest. You can invite your friends by email or through social accounts. Only those who have the code to a private contest can join that contest.

How do I create a private contest?

Follow these simple steps to unravel the fun together with your friends:

- Select the match that you want to participate in

- Click on the ‘Create own contest’ button

- Create the contest of your choice using the given options & join

- Invite your friends via email, or by sharing a unique code on Social accounts

- Your friends can join this contest by clicking on the link sent to them or They can enter the provide code in that match ‘enter contest code’ option.

Now you have your own private contest on MyBat11.

How do I join a private contest?

- You will receive a link or a contest code to join a private contest on MyBat11 if invited by your friend. Click on this link or enter the contest code by clicking on ‘Enter contest code’ option in that match contest page.

- If you are a registered user on MyBat11 and haven’t already created a team for the match, you will need to create your team for the contest you’re invited to

- After creating your team, enter the invitation code on the contests page. Click on the ‘Join’ button. The system will confirm deduction of the entry fee for the contest from your account or prompt you to make payment in case your account does not have the adequate balance.

- Make the payment for the entry fee to the invited contest

- Join more contests to win more cash!

How do I join contests?

To join contests, click on the ‘Join’ button along the specific contest you wish to join.

What are cash & practice contest?

Cash contests are where you participate by paying an entry fee and compete with other users to win real money! The prize money will be awarded to the user who has the highest points in the contest.In a practice contest, you compete with other users for bragging rights. Unlike cash contests, there will be no entry fee for this contest. A practice contest will help you boost yourfantasy cricket skill. Once you are confident with your selection skill, you can join cash contests.

How is the prize money distributed in case of multiple winners?

The prize money that each winner will receive is explained in detail on clicking the 'Winners' link for that contest. Please note that taxes will be deducted as applicable on winnings above Rs. 10,000 as per government regulations.

What happens if there is a tie between winners of a multiple winner contest?

In case of a tie between winners of a multiple winner contest for any position, the prize money for the winner's position and the immediately next winning position (if any), and so forth in accordance with the number of tied winners, will be aggregated and shared equally among the tied winners. All other prize money will remain as per the winner's standing.

For example: If there are 2 winners tied for the 1st position, the sum of the 1st and 2nd position prize money will be distributed equally between these 2 winners and the next highest scorer will be awarded the prize money for the 3rd position.

What happens if an ODI/T20 match is abandoned?

If the real match is abandoned fantasy match will be cancelled and the entry fees will be refunded. This includes scenarios where the real match is abandoned without a single ball being bowled, as well as where the match is abandoned after it has started.

What happens if a test match is declared as drawn?

If the real test match is declared as drawn with neither of the teams completing a single inning, the fantasy match will be cancelled and the entry fees will be refunded.

If the real test match is declared drawn with either team (and not necessarily both) completing a single inning, the fantasy match will be considered to have completed and the entry fees will not be refunded.

Notes:

An innings will be deemed to be completed if a batting team either declares or gets bowled out (i.e., loses ten wickets) in such innings.

What if the contest doesn’t fill up with required number of participants?

In case of a cash contest, the contest will be cancelled and the entry fee will be refunded to your account. But where’s the fun in that? So, make sure to invite maximum friends to join the contest and fill the required slots!

What do I win in a practice contest?

You’ve got to pay a little cash to win more cash in return. Practice contests do not have any cash prizes and are meant for those who want to gain experience in playing and boost their skill.

Can I get my friend(s) to join the same contest in which I am playing?

There is nothing better than friends competing against each other in the same contest! That’s why we have private contests wherein you can invite your friends through email or you can share a unique code on your or Facebook wall or social media accounts to participate in the same contest.

Can I join future matches and contests in advance?

Considering the excitement and passion of cricket fans, we allow our users to pre-select their teams for all the matches that are open on MyBat11 at any given point of time.

Can I view other users' teams in a contest?

Sure! But to maintain the element of surprise, we allow users to view other users’ teams only after the deadline hits. To view your opponent’s team, go to the ‘Joined contests’ tab for a match and click on your opponent’s team name. You can also track your competitors by downloading the details of other teams in that contest.

Do I have to create a separate team for each Match Contest?

Yes! You are required to create a separate team for each new match contest.

How do you decide the winner of a contest?

The team which gets the highest points at the end of the match will be announced as the winner of that contest.

ACCOUNT BALANCE:

How it Works?

You can view, deposit or withdraw money into your MyBat11 account anytime by going to “My Account”.

Remember, you need to get your account verified by us to withdraw money!

Don’t worry. This is a one-time activity unless you want to change your account details.

Your MyBat11 account is divided into 3 categories:

1 - Deposited: If you have deposited any amount but haven’t joined any contest using this amount, it will be categorized as ‘Unutilized or deposited’ in your account

2 - Winnings: The amount you have earned by winning contests. No processing fees will be deducted if you wish to withdraw any amount from your winnings

3 - Cash Bonus: It’s a freebie! This is a bonus amount given to you that cannot be withdrawn. However, you can use it to join more contests. Cash Bonus also comes with an expiry date, so use it before it expires!

What is Cash Bonus? How can I get it?

Check out our ‘Invite Friends’/bounty page for further details on how you can earn a cash bonus. Remember, you can use your cash bonus to join any public cash contests but you won’t be able to join any private contests with the same. You can also earn cash bonus through our promotions and offers or reward program. Your cash bonus expires from 60 days of credit.

What if my account balance is not correct?

Hmmm...That's rare. But if you require any help regarding your MyBat11 account balance or any other account-related queries, feel free to reach us from the Contact Us page

How long does it take for my deposited amount to be credited into my MyBat11 account balance?

Once you have made the deposit, your MyBat11 account balance will be updated maximum within 20 minutes!

In what order does my Account Balance get utilized towards the entry fee for a cash contest?

When you join a public cash contest, MyBat11 shall first debit the entry fee amount from your 'Cash Bonus' account (If Bonus allowed), then from your Unutilized/deposited account, followed by your Winnings account to cover the entry fee for any cash contest that you join.

When you join a private cash contest, MyBat11 shall first debit the entry fee amount from your 'Unutilized/deposited' account, followed by your Winnings account to cover the entry fee for any cash contest that you join.

VERIFICAITONS :

Is verification necessary to join contests on MyBat11?

No. You can join contests even without verifying your account. When you win cash contests, your winnings will be deposited in your MyBat11 account. Verification becomes mandatory only before you can make withdrawals from your MyBat11 account to your bank account.

How do I get my MyBat11 account verified?

Account verification is a one-time process. For your account to be verified, click on the ‘Account Verification’ link on ‘Me’ page.. Follow the steps mentioned below to get your MyBat11 account verified:

1 - Verify your mobile number by entering the OTP received

2 - Verify your email by entering the email id.

3 - Upload your PAN Card number, PAN Card image along with other details & and submit the form.

4 - Your PAN Card verification will be completed within 7 working days. Additionally, we recommend that you submit your bank account details after submitting the PAN details to ensure that your complete account verification gets done in one go.

Why did my verification fail?

Some of the major reasons why your verification request may have failed are:

1 - PAN Card (mandatory) is not uploaded as id proof for verification

* Your withdrawals on MyBat11 involve transfer of money. Hence, PAN Card is mandatory to get your MyBat11 account verified. Please upload a clear image of your PAN Card.

2 - PAN number on the card does not match with the PAN number entered during verification request

* Double check the PAN number that you have entered to avoid errors

3 - Date of Birth on the PAN Card does not match with the Date of Birth on your MyBat11 account

* Please ensure that you enter the exact Date of Birth on the verification request, as it appears on the PAN Card

4 - Name on the PAN Card does not match with the name on the bank account

* You can withdraw money from your MyBat11 account only to your own bank account. Hence, the name on the PAN Card and bank account needs to match.

5 - IFSC code provided in the bank details is incorrect/belongs to a different bank branch than the one mentioned for verification purposes.

* If you provide the wrong IFSC code, your money could be transferred to someone else's account. So, make sure you enter the correct code. You can find the IFSC code in your cheque book, passbook or bank statements. If you're not sure, please contact your bank branch to get the correct code.

6 - The documents uploaded by you are not clear/visible

* Our experts are humans after all! Please help them by uploading clear documents so that they can verify your details quickly.

7 - Bank account number entered in verification request does not match with account number appearing on the uploaded proof.

* You don't want your hard earned money going to someone else's account right? Neither do we! So please ensure that you enter the exact bank account number in the verification request, as it appears on the bank account proof. In the rare case that your verification request gets rejected, the reason for rejection will also appear on top of the verification page for your reference.

Do I have to pay taxes when I withdraw money to my bank account?

Taxes are deducted at source wherever applicable and paid out by us, as per the Income Tax Act 1961. Winners will be provided TDS certificates in respect of such tax deductions, if applicable. However, you shall be responsible for payment of any other applicable tax, including but not limited to, income tax, gift tax, etc. in respect of the Cash Prizes money won. If you have any queries regarding taxation, we strongly suggest that you consult with a tax expert. For more information please check our Terms and Conditions.

What are the taxes applicable if I win more than Rs.10,000?

As per the Income Tax Act 1961, if your individual winnings for a single contest are more than Rs.10,000, tax of 31.2% will be deducted. The remaining amount (68.8%) will be credited to your winnings balance. For e.g. If you win Rs.1,00,000, tax of Rs.31,200 will be deducted and your actual winnings are Rs.68,800.
